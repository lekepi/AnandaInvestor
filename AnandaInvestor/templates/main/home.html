About Ananda

Ananda Asset Management is a London-based equity investment manager investing in Europe and North America. Ananda was founded in 2018 by Louis Villa and Araceli Strassburger, who have over 30 years of combined experience in public markets. Please visit the Team section for more information. We invest our internal assets, along with those of a diverse group of family offices, high net worth individuals and financial institutions, providing a perfect alignment of interest. Ananda is authorised and regulated by the FCA.
